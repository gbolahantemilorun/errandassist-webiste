Placing Order

Fill the order form or send the order details to +2348035157700 via text or whatsapp. Order Details include Fullname, Telephone, Errand Description, Delivery Address and Delivery Date.

Quotation/Order Confirmation

Receive a quote for your errand order from ErrandAssist and confirm your order.

Order Processing/Delivery

ErrandAssist processes your order and delivers at your doorstep between 12noon and 6pm. Office/Business deliveries are done between 12noon and 4pm.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have been able to provide answers to the questions we regularly receive from clients. If you need further clarification, please reach out to us via our contact details.

  • Is your service charge of NGN500 a flat rate for all goods errands?

    Yes. The service charge applies to goods errands and it is per errand order. Everything stated in the errand order will be fulfilled at the rate of NGN500. Service errands attract custom charges.

  • What does your delivery cost look like?

    Delivery cost depends on the weight of the goods and the delivery location. Once you place your order, the quote you receive from ErrandAssist will cover the cost of fulfilling your errands, the service charge and the delivery cost.

  • Are there specific locations that you deliver to?

    Yes. Our delivery is within Abuja and suburbs for now.

  • What if my errand is not included in your list of errands?

    Just put a call through to us on +2348035157700 or send us a text/whatsapp message.

  • What does your service errand look like?

    1. Fill the order form or send the order details to +2348035157700 via text or whatsapp. Order Details include Fullname, Telephone, Service Description, Service Location and Fulfillment Date;
    2. Receive a quote and reach an agreement with ErrandAssist on the cost implication of the service to be provided;
    3. Mobilize ErrandAssist with at least 50% of the total service cost;
    4. ErrandAssist deploys service provider to fulfill your order.
    5. On 50% completion, pay balance to ErrandAssist.
